Juice Cleanses

Reset, reboot, renew

Each 3-day cleanse comes with fifteen 16oz juices — raw, unpasteurized, and packed with vitamins, minerals, and active enzymes. That's roughly three pounds of fresh produce per bottle.

Consume at 2–3 hour intervals throughout the day. Drink at least eight glasses of filtered water alongside your juices. First-time cleansers may eat approved snacks if needed.

How it works

  1. Choose your cleanse and schedule a pickup time via Calendly. Payment is collected during booking.
  2. Pick up your juices on Wednesday, Friday, or Sunday. We need 48 hours notice minimum.
  3. Start cleansing on Thursday, Saturday, or Monday. Drink one juice every 2–3 hours, with the last one 2 hours before bed.
  4. Ease back in with salads, smoothies, soups, and gluten-free grains. Your body will thank you.

Shelf life is 4 days from pickup — start immediately. Drink 8+ glasses of water daily alongside your juices.
